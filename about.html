About Us...

Our aim here at The Road Less Travelled, is to highlight the amazing destinations on offer throughout Hungary. We have handpicked our favourite locations, all with the potential to provide you with your ultimate dream holiday! Explore the maps on each of the destinations to see what wonderful amenities are available in the area, from accommodation options, to restaurant suggestions and the must-see tourist attractions. So head on over to our destinations page to find your dream location!
