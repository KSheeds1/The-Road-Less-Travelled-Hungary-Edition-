Found on the western edge of Lake Balaton, Keszthely offers a more laid-back, lake-side retreat than other towns found around Lake Balaton. If you're looking to relax by the water without the crowds, take in some culture and enjoy a lively yet relaxed night out and about, look no further than Keszthely. Wander down Fó Ter and you'll find yourself happening upon the Festetics Palace, take some time to explore the palace and the beautiful grounds it's set on. We also recommend taking a day trip to Hévís, only 5km from Keszthely and home to the largest thermal lake in the world. Take a dip in its healing waters and don't say we didn't warn you about the smell!
