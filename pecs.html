Located in the south-west of Hungary in the foothills of the Mecsek mountains, lies the city of Pécs. Despite being the fifth largest city in Hungary, Pécs has all the charm and atmosphere of a small town, making it the the perfect destination for a quiet getaway. In saying this, that does not mean that you won't have plenty to do while visiting! A UNESCO World Heritage site, Pécs dates back more than 2,000 years and it's history and architecture must be experienced. Kiraly Utca is the perfect spot for some shopping and lunch, we reccommend you take a seat outside one of the many restaurants and people watch. Spend a day in the Zsolnay Cultural quarter, take in the museums and explore the grounds. So if you're looking for an alternative to Budapest, or the allure of the capital has worn off, make youy way to Pécs - you won't be disappointed!
