Siófok is a charming town located on the southern bank of Lake Balaton, as Hungary is land locked, most hungarians flock to Lake Balaton during the summer months to enjoy the rays by the lake. Popular with both natives and tourists alike due to the abundance of water-based activities offered and it's rather wild nightlife! Siófok is best when visited during the summer months, to beat the crowds, we reccommend visiting in May. It's location makes it the perfect base for exploring the surrounding towns, either side of the lake! We reccommend taking the ferry to Thiany to see the lavender fields! Home to beautiful beaches, a great selection of restaurants and bars, (that's not even counting the strip!) water games, and music and entertainment Siófok has all you need for some fun in the sun!
